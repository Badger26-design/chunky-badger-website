About Chunky Badger

Making Learning to Draw Feel Like Play

At Chunky Badger, we believe every child has the potential to be a budding artist. Our books are designed for beginners and parents looking for creative, screen-free fun.


We don't just show you the finished picture; we guide you through the process. Our "6 Simple Steps" method breaks down complex subjects into easy-to-follow shapes. We provide plenty of space to practice, shade, and get creative right inside each book.


It’s not just a book; it’s an art adventure that builds confidence with every page turn!

Now Available: Learn to Draw: British Animals

Discover the charm of British wildlife! This step-by-step guide helps young artists master over 45 woodland creatures, farm favorites, and seaside friends.

Inside you’ll find:

  • Woodland Creatures: From curious Foxes to cuddly Badgers.
  • Farm Favourites: Playful Pigs, Sheep, and the majestic Highland Cow.
  • Seaside Friends: Draw the perfect Puffin, Seagull, and even an Otter!

Why Parents Love It:

  • Easy to Follow: Every animal is broken down into 6 visual steps—no reading required!
  • All-in-One: Includes practice space and tips on how to start with basic shapes.
  • Creative Freedom: Once the outline is done, we encourage kids to add colour to their masterpiece!

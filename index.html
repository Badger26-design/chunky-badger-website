About Chunky Badger

Making Learning to Draw Feel Like Play

At Chunky Badger, we believe every child has the potential to be a budding artist. Our books are designed for beginners and parents looking for creative, screen-free fun.


We don't just show you the finished picture; we guide you through the process. Our "6 Simple Steps" method breaks down complex subjects into easy-to-follow shapes. We provide plenty of space to practice, shade, and get creative right inside each book.


It's not just a book; it's an art adventure that builds confidence with every page turn!

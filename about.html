Our Story

Every Child is a Budding Artist

At Chunky Badger, we believe that creativity should be accessible, fun, and completely screen-free. Our books are designed for beginners and parents looking for creative ways to spend quality time together.


We do not just show you the finished picture; we guide you through the process. Our "6 Simple Steps" method breaks down complex subjects into easy-to-follow shapes. We provide plenty of space to practice, shade, and get creative right inside each book.


It is not just a book; it is an art adventure that builds confidence with every page turn. Whether it is a rainy day or a quiet afternoon, Chunky Badger is here to spark imagination.
